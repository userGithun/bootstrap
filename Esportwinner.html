Our new player for BMPS 2024

TX Joker (Khush Singh)

TX Joker,(JokerOP) is a popular esports player in the BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India) scene, representing Team X Spark (TX). Known for his exceptional gameplay, strategic skill, and accuracy, Joker has become one of the key players on the team. His expertise in close-range combat and quick decision-making are essential in high-stakes tournaments, and his calm demeanor under pressure has made him a fan favorite in the BGMI esports community.

Achievements

  • Sharp Shooting: TX Joker is renowned for his exceptional shooting skills in BGMI tournaments.
  • Quick Decision-Making: His ability to make fast, strategic choices enhances his gameplay.
  • Calm Under Pressure: Joker excels in high-stress situations, contributing to his effectiveness as a player.
TX Joker recently joined Team X Spark from Team SouL to compete in tournaments like BMPS 2024.

Coach of Team X Spark

Osmium (Rahul Sharma)

The coach of Team X Spark for the 2024 BGIS (Battlegrounds Mobile India Series) season is Rahul "Osmium" Sharma. Osmium’s strategic expertise has been instrumental in helping Team X Spark improve their performance and tactics, especially in overcoming challenges during the BGIS 2024 qualifiers and advancing to the tournament’s finals ​
