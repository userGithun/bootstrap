FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Esports Champ Cup Closing Ceremony

Where and when will the closing ceremony take place?

The Closing Ceremony will be held on Sunday, 25th of September near Gwalior City, offering ticket holders access to The Venue and Fan Zone. You can find the exact location here.

What experiences are available at The Venue? When does it open?

The ceremony will feature live streams of the pre-show, Esports Champ Cup Club Trophy Awarding and Trophy journey as well as the Esports Champ Cup Celebration of Champions, high-tech stage shows, and performances by Kid Cudi, Steve Aoki, Rabeh Saqer, and SEVDALIZA. The Venue will be open from 7:00 PM until approximately 12.30 AM.

What will visitors find in the Fan Zone? When does it open?

Enjoy pre-show and post-show fun in the Fan Zone, where you can savor a variety of food and beverages, relax in the comfortable hangout area, and browse exclusive ECC merchandise and engaging partner booths. Open from 7:00 PM until late, the Fan Zone is the perfect spot to immerse yourself in the closing ceremony’s vibrant atmosphere. Please note, it will briefly close during the Esports Champ Cup Celebration of Champions.

Will the closing ceremony be available for live streaming?

Yes, parts of the closing ceremony will be available for live streaming, including the pre-show, Esports Champ Cup Club Trophy Awarding, trophy journey, and Esports Champ Cup Celebration of Champions.

What additional experiences can I enjoy on the day of the closing ceremony?

On the day of the Esports Champ Cup Closing Ceremony, the ECC will be open for its final day. Feel free to drop by earlier to explore our festival venues or watch the esports tournament finals.

What are the parking, drop-off and pickup options at the event?

For details on parking and drop-off/pickup options,view the event map here. It shows the locations for Public Parking and VIP Parking.

General Information

What is the Esports Champ Cup?

The Esports Champ Cup (ECC) is a top-tier esports event held annually in Hyderabad. The first ECC will run from July to September 2024 at a cutting-edge venue in Hyderabad City. Featuring the largest prize pool in esports history and popular game titles, the ECC offers a unique cross-game competition format, rewards for both individual titles and team performances, and includes qualifiers, live entertainment, and fan activities.

Where is the Esports Champ Cup?

ECC is hosted in Hyderabad City, Riyadh’s entertainment district, at a newly constructed world-class venue.

When is the Esports Champ Cup?

The Esports Champ Cup 2024 will be held from July 3 to September 25, 2024.

What can I expect at the Esports Champ Cup?

Get ready for a whirlwind of excitement! Expect epic esports tournaments, thrilling community contests, game challenges, high-speed races, exclusive meet-and-greets, dazzling exhibitions, unforgettable shows, cool merchandise, electrifying concerts, and so much more!

How is the Esports Champ Cup organized?

The Esports Champ Cup is split into two main areas: the Esports District and the Gaming District. These districts are linked by various interactive activations and experiences, making it easy for visitors to explore and enjoy everything the event has to offer.

Tournaments

What can participants expect from the ECC Tournaments?

Participants in the ECC Tournaments can anticipate an exciting and challenging environment with eight weeks of intense competition. They will face top-tier players and clubs in high-stakes matches, all while competing for a share of the impressive $60 million prize pool.

What can viewers expect from the ECC Tournaments?

Viewers can look forward to eight weeks of captivating and high-energy gameplay in the ECC Tournaments. The action will feature the best games and elite players from around the world, providing thrilling entertainment and dramatic moments as competitors battle for a massive $60 million prize pool.

Where can I watch the tournaments?

Tournaments can be viewed at designated ECC arenas and through our official online streaming platforms.

Where can I watch online streaming?

Tournaments are available on multiple online platforms, visit our Where to Watch page for detailed streaming information for each game.

Are there any opportunities for players to qualify for the ECC Tournament at the last minute?

Yes, there are Last Chance Qualifiers available! These events offer players a final opportunity to earn a spot in an ECC Tournament. They will take place at the STC Play Gaming Hall for select games. For more details and to find out how to participate, please visit the stc play website.

Festival

What can I expect to experience at the festival?

The festival offers a rich array of activities tailored to all age groups and interests. From family-friendly events to specialized attractions, there's something for everyone. Explore the Festival Page to ensure you don't miss out!
What are the daily opening and closing times for the festival?
The festival and most venues open daily at 6 PM and close at 2 AM. Note that Powr presented by HMG and Falcon HQ close at 11 PM.

Are there specific days when any venues are closed?

All venues are open daily, with the following exceptions: Powr presented by HMG is closed on Tuesdays, Falcon HQ is closed on Mondays, and EWC Takes Over House of Hype is closed on Sundays and Mondays.

Which festival venues offer free entry?

You can explore and enjoy unlimited entertainment at the following venues for free: stc play Gaming Hall, Saudi Esports Legacy Museum, Saudi Esports Academy, Aramco SIM Arena, Jameel Motorsport Park, EVA, and ECC Takes Over House of Hype. Please note that while entry is free, some specific in-venue activities may have associated costs.

What stage entertainment can I enjoy during the ECC?

During the ECC, you can enjoy a variety of shows and activities. Catch spectacular drone shows every Friday, dazzling fireworks on Sundays, and daily performances and concerts on the main stage. Additionally, explore the venues and their unique stage activations for more exciting experiences.

Are there festival events suitable for kids?

The festival is packed with exciting activities for kids, from helmet painting and quest map stamp collections to thrilling family quizzes and exclusive Powr/Falcon meet-and-greets, among many other fun experiences. With so much to do, it is a perfect opportunity to create unforgettable memories!

Ticketing

What ticket do I need to enter everything?

Get your All Access Pass that grants you unlimited entry to all festival venues, both ticketed and non-ticketed, with the added benefit of fast-track entry for a seamless EWC experience. Please note that the pass is valid for one festival day only and does not cover any additional costs for in-venue activities.

What tickets do I need to watch a tournament, and where can I get them?

To watch a tournament, you will need a specific ticket for arena access. You can purchase these tickets in advance through Webook or buy them directly on-site at the Amazon Arena, Qiddiya Arena, or stc Arena.

Which festival venues require a ticket for entry, and where can I get these tickets?m?

The following festival venues require a ticket for entry: Powr presented by HMG, Falcon HQ, and Anime Park.

Other Information

What is the New Global Sport Conference?

The ECC conference, known as NGSC24, is a major event that brings together leaders from sports, esports, gaming, entertainment, and technology to explore new global opportunities in these fields. It builds on the Next World Forum series and NGSC23, offering a comprehensive platform for industry professionals to connect and collaborate.

What is the closing ceremony?

The closing ceremony, held on August 25th, will be a grand finale celebrating the end of the Esports Champ Cup. It will include awards, a festival atmosphere, fireworks, a light show, live concerts, and more surprises.

Do kids need to be accompanied by an adult?

Yes, children under the age of 14 must be accompanied by an adult with a valid ticket.
