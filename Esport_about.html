ABOUT
BATTLEGROUND'S MOBILE INDIA

FORMAT

ROUND 1

 128 invited teams will be divided into two sets: Blue and Red. Each set will be further divided into 4 groups of 16 teams each. Round 1 will span over two weeks and after week 1 matches, teams will be reshuffled into Blue & Red sets based on overall standings. The reshuffled sets will play in the same format as week 1. The top 64 teams based on the overall standings after both weeks will move to Round 2.

ROUND 2

 The top 64 teams from Round 1 will be divided into 4 groups of 16 teams each, with each group playing 6 matches. Top 48 teams based on the overall ranking will advance to Round 3.

ROUND 3

 48 teams from Round 2 will be divided into 3 groups of 16 teams each. Each team will play 4 matches across 2 days. After that, the teams will be reshuffled into new groups based on the overall points standings and will play in the same format for the next 2 days. The top 24 teams based on the cumulative standings will qualify for the semi-finals.

SEMI-FINALS

 The 24 qualifying teams from Round 3 will be divided into 3 groups of 8 where teams will play 4 rounds of double round-robin matches. Each team will play 4 matches per day and a total of 16 matches. The top 16 teams from the overall standings will advance to the Grand Finals.

GRAND-FINALS

 The 16 remaining teams will compete in this high-stakes LAN event and will play a total of 18 matches over three days. The team that finishes at the top spot of the points table will be crowned the champions of BMPS 2024.
